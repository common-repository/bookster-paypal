Accept PayPal online payments at Bookster checkout

== Description ==

This **Bookster PayPal Addon** integrates PayPal into your Bookster checkout experience. 

Now you can accept secure payments directly from your Bookster's booking form, making it easier than ever to collect payment for bookings, reservations, 1-1 appointments, online courses, or any other service you offer.

**NOTE: A [free version of Bookster 1.1.0 or later](https://wordpress.org/plugins/bookster/) is required to use this plugin.**

== Features ==

**Here's what you get:**

- **Effortless Setup**: No coding required! Install and activate the addon in just a few clicks. Then, fill out your PayPal's Client ID and Client Secret.
- **Seamless Integration**: The plugin integrates flawlessly with your existing Bookster forms, ensuring a smooth checkout experience for your customers.
- **Secure Transactions**: Leverage the trusted security of PayPal to protect your customer information and transactions.
- **Multiple Payment Options**: Through the PayPal gateway, you can take payments from credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal balance. It's also possible to activate the PayPal option along with paying later with cash.

**This Bookster addon is perfect for:**

- **Bookster users** offering classes, workshops, appointments, or hourly rentals.
- **Businesses** accepting reservations or booking **deposits**.
- Anyone looking to simplify **online payments** through their Bookster forms.

== Installation ==
Manual installation is easy and takes less than one minute.

1. Search for "Bookster PayPal" and install this plugin.
2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins‘ menu in WordPress.
3. Go to the **Bookster** > **Addons** menu to set up your PayPal credentials.

You’re done. Enjoy!
